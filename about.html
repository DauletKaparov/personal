About Me

I'm Daulet Kaparov, a rising sophomore at the University of Pennsylvania double-majoring in Computer Science and Economics. As an Asset Analyst intern at Atlas Capital, I blend data-driven financial modeling with hands-on field research to uncover actionable investment insights.

On the technical side, I've built everything from Chrome extensions to scalable web apps and AI-powered data-science utilities—always with an eye toward empowering others. My passion lies at the intersection of technology and finance, where I can leverage data to drive meaningful decisions.

Outside of work and code, you'll find me sparring in the boxing ring, backpacking across Central Asia in search of new perspectives, or experimenting as an amateur Turkish-cuisine chef in my kitchen. I'm passionate about continuous learning, whether it's mastering a new programming language or exploring different cultures through their cuisines.
